Inititives

Specific actions are researched, planned and promoted within the school community via meaningful student lead interest groups, curriculum, management and day to day operations of the school. Students decision-making drives change for sustainable outcomes. Teams of students supported by staff mentors are empowered to lead and sustain initiatives developed through learning. Ensure social inclusion to enable members of the school community to participate fully in school life while instilling a long-lasting respect for human rights, freedoms, cultures and creative expression. Endeavor to practice global citizenship, enriching our educational mission with activities and partnerships that improve the lives of people living in other parts of the world.

Encourage an interactive process of becoming aware of and practicing healthy choices to create a more successful and sustainable lifestyle nurturing people and nature, now and in the future. Promoting awareness of multiple aspects of wellbeing including emotional, physical, social, environmental, occupational, spiritual and intellectual.
